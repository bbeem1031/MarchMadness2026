Methodology — The 15 Rules
01–05
● Mandatory

Always pick an 11-seed, always pick a 12-seed

11 vs 6 is the most common upset (33 times). At-large 11-seeds win 64.1%. 12 vs 5 is nearly a coinflip at 43.8%.

64.1% at-large 11-seed win rate
03
● Mandatory

Screen seeds 5–8 for BARTHAG below 0.85

Seeds 5–8 with BARTHAG under 0.85 lose 61.4% of first-round games. Your fastest single-metric upset filter.

61.4% upset rate in danger zone
04–05
● Mandatory

Fade favorites traveling 1,000+ miles or crossing 2 time zones

1,000mi trips: 38.5% loss rate (+14pts above baseline). 2 time zones crossed: 54.5% — more than double baseline.

54.5% fav loss rate, 2 TZ crossing
06
● Strong

Never blindly pick a 6-seed to the Sweet 16

The 6-seed has the worst PASE of any seed at -20.3. Only a 50% first-round win rate despite being treated as safe.

-20.3 PASE — worst of all seeds
07
● Strong

Check if the underdog was AP Top 25 this season

Seeds 9–13 that spent time in the AP Poll win 46.7% vs 28.3% for those never ranked. Biggest non-efficiency predictor.

+18.4pp win rate boost
08–09
● Strong

Trust Big Ten & Pac-12 dogs · Fade Big 12 & MWC seeds

Big Ten +4.5 PASE, Pac-12 +7.3 — chronically underseeded. Big 12 -15.9 PASE, MWC -15.6 — inflated by brand.

Big 12 -15.9 PASE — worst conference
